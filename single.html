DispatchWaves Express To Uganda

DispatchWaves is pleased to engage on an express delivery this year to the African nation of Uganda, on the 16 March 2023. Team Uganda is looking forward to our presence on express-vanza.
Visit us at Hall ED, stand #23, where our team of experts will be delighted to offer guidance and tailor-made solutions for all your reefer and consumables transportation and logistics needs.
Billions of tons of reefers including sorghum are transported to and fro in Uganda every year, which represents a high percentage of the global market, and DispatchWaves stands out as a trusted partner of producers' nation - Uganda.


DispatchWaves in Seafood Global

DispatchWaves is delighted to be a participant of the Seafood Expo 2023, which is the world’s largest seafood trade event, taking place from 25-27 April in Barcelona. Our reefer experts will be at your disposal.
Our reefer experts will be at your disposal to discuss your needs and offer logistics solutions that best suit your business, no matter the type of seafood you transport or the complexity of your supply chain. DispatchWaves unrivalled customer care and expertise in the global transport of refrigerated cargoes makes us the perfect partner for your seafood shipments.
These experts are continuously trained to meet the growing reefer market demands of the fish and seafood market, so you can be sure your cargo is in safe hands.
